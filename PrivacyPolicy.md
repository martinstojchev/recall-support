Recall – Privacy Policy
Effective Date: December 2025
App: Recall – Clipboard Manager
Data Collection
Recall stores your clipboard history locally on your Mac.
No data is uploaded to any server.
No user data is shared with third parties.
Tracking
Recall does not track your activity.
Third-Party Services
Recall does not use any analytics or external services that collect your data.
User Control
You can clear your clipboard history at any time.
All data remains on your device unless you manually delete it.
